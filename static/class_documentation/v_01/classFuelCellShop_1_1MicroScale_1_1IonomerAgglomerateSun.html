Detailed Description

Analytical solution to an ionomer-filled agglomerate problem in 1D.

Adapted from Source: W. Sun, B. A. Peppley, K. Karan, An improved two-dimensional agglomerate cathode model to study the influence of catalyst layer structural parameters, Electrochimica Acta 50 (16-17) (2005) 33473358.
